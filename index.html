CASCADE FORCE

Cinematic 2.5D WebGL run-and-gun — bloom lighting, steroid-tier weapons, mid-bosses, and escalating war machines. Duck under fire. Aim skyward. Inspired by classic genre DNA — built as an original game.

  • AD / Arrows — Move
  • W / ↑ — Aim up (diagonal with move)
  • Space / K — Jump
  • S / ↓ — Duck (dodge high shots) · aim down in air
  • J / Z — Fire
  • Enter — Start / Continue
